Seniority is our Priority

The project of LIONS CHARITABLE TRUST is named as “LIONS SENIOR CITIZENS PARADISE” Home is located in a natural environment and is close to the city with serene atmosphere throughout the day, with lots of greenery aroma.

Lions Airport city Old Age Home is an activity of Bangalore Airport city Lions Service Trust. The basic concept of the old age home is to provide simple and peaceful living atmosphere to the senior citizens.
